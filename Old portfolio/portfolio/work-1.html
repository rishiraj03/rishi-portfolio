Audiobook and Podcasting App

Problem Statement:

I want to add a “highlight the audio” option so that users would be able to save the section of their choice. The App which I will be designing will contain Audiobooks, as well as Podcasts where everyone, will be able to add and consume content. The Unique aspect of this design would be the “highlight option” which will be helpful. And this would provide flexibility to the users.
